U Thant

Third secretary-general of the United Nations

A Timeline of U Thant's life:

  • 1909: Born in Pantanaw, Myanmar(formerly known as Burma).
  • 1957: Became Burma's permanent UN representative.
  • 1959: Became vice president of the UN Generaly Assembly.
  • 1962: Was elected permanent secretary general after the death of UN Secretary General Dag Hammarskjold.
  • 1966: Was re-elected for five years.
  • 1974: Died in New York, United State.
"U Thant has put the world deeply in his debt."
— John F. Kennedy, October 1962
